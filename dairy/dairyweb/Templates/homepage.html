About us

At the forefront of Surbhi Dairy is the exceptional and visionary figure, Prof. S. G. Mane. As the founder and driving force behind this remarkable enterprise, Prof. Mane embodies the essence of a true trailblazer. With a spirit fueled by passion and an unwavering commitment to excellence, he has established Surbhi Dairy as a paragon of innovation and quality in the dairy industry. Prof. Mane's journey began with an insatiable desire to revolutionize the way dairy products are produced and consumed. Inspired by a profound reverence for nature and a deep-rooted respect for traditional farming practices, he embarked on a quest to create a harmonious blend of tradition and technology. Under his astute leadership, Surbhi Dairy has emerged as a beacon of sustainability, championing eco-conscious practices that prioritize the welfare of animals and the preservation of the environment.
